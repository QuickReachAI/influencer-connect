# QuickConnects - Feature Overview 🌟

## 🎭 Dual User Experiences

### For Brands 🏢

#### Discovery & Search
- **Find Perfect Influencers**: Search by niche, followers, engagement rate
- **Advanced Filtering**: Location, platform, budget compatibility
- **Detailed Profiles**: View portfolio, past collaborations, rate cards
- **Smart Recommendations**: Algorithm-suggested matches

#### Campaign Management
- **Create Proposals**: Define deliverables, budget, timeline
- **Track Status**: Monitor pending, active, completed deals
- **Deliverables Tracking**: Checkbox list of requirements
- **Historical Archive**: Review past collaborations

#### Communication
- **Direct Messaging**: Chat with influencers about campaigns
- **Thread Organization**: Messages organized by deal
- **Real-time Updates**: See latest communications

#### Dashboard Analytics
- **Active Deals Counter**: Current running campaigns
- **Pending Proposals**: Awaiting influencer response
- **Quick Stats**: Total reach, engagement metrics
- **Recent Activity**: Latest updates and actions

---

### For Influencers 📸

#### Brand Discovery
- **Browse Opportunities**: Filter by industry, budget range
- **Company Profiles**: View brand details, requirements, past campaigns
- **Budget Transparency**: See compensation ranges upfront
- **Industry Filtering**: Find brands in your niche

#### Deal Management
- **Review Offers**: See all incoming proposals
- **Accept/Decline**: One-click offer management
- **Negotiate Terms**: Counter-offer via messaging
- **Track Progress**: Monitor deal lifecycle

#### Portfolio Showcase
- **Social Stats**: Display follower counts across platforms
- **Engagement Rates**: Show your performance metrics
- **Past Collaborations**: Highlight successful campaigns
- **Rate Card**: Transparent pricing for different content types

#### Earnings Dashboard
- **Total Revenue**: Track completed campaign earnings
- **Active Deals Value**: See upcoming payments
- **Deal History**: Review all past collaborations
- **Performance Metrics**: Engagement, reach, conversions
