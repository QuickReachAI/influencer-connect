Guidelines

How to Deliver These Scripts

Make it yours. Here's how to get the best results from each format.

Use Your Own Voice

These are starting points, not teleprompter scripts. Reword everything to match how you naturally talk on camera. The message stays — the delivery is yours.

Keep It Authentic

Don't read from a script on camera. Understand the 3 key points and speak naturally. If you've had bad brand deal experiences, sharing that makes it more relatable.

Stay Platform-Native

Use trending audio for Reels. Use interactive stickers (polls, questions, links) for Stories. Use carousel slides with clear visuals for Posts.

Always Include the CTA

Every piece of content must direct people to the sign-up form — either via link in bio, link sticker in stories, or a clear verbal call-to-action.

Format Cheat Sheet

Reel

30-45 seconds. Hook in first 2 sec. Talking head + text overlays. Use trending audio. Best for: reach.

Story Series

3-4 slides. Start with poll. End with link + share sticker. Best for: engagement + direct sign-ups.

Post / Carousel

Long caption with detail. Pair with carousel slides or screenshot. Best for: saves + shares.

Do's and Don'ts

  • Do mention escrow payments — it's the #1 thing that resonates with creators
  • Do emphasize that all creator sizes are welcome (nano to macro)
  • Do share your own brand deal frustrations if you have them — relatability wins
  • Don't make it sound like an ad — keep it conversational and genuine
  • Don't overpromise earnings or guaranteed brand deals
  • Don't skip the CTA — every post must point to the sign-up form
