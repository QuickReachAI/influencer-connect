Guidelines & Tips

Share these pointers with your influencer partners so they get the best results.

For Influencers

Key Points to Always Hit

No matter which format, these 3 things must come through:

1. Escrow-Protected Payments

Money is locked before work begins — creators always get paid.

2. All Creator Sizes Welcome

1K or 100K — doesn't matter. Every niche, every audience size.

3. Fill Out the Form

Clear CTA — link in bio/stories to the Google Form.

WhatsApp Outreach

WhatsApp Best Practices

Personalize

Mention something specific about their content — "loved your recent skincare reel"

Keep it Short

WhatsApp is not email. 4-5 lines max per message.

Respond Fast

If they reply, respond within a few hours. Speed = trust.

Don't Spam

2 follow-ups max. If no response after that, let it go.

Match Their Language

If they respond in Hindi or Hinglish, match that energy.

Be Human

Don't sound like a template. Adapt the messages to feel natural.

Tone

Script Tone & Adaptation

These scripts are written casual and Gen-Z friendly. Influencers should:

Use Their Own Voice

Reword to match how they naturally talk on camera. The message stays, the delivery is theirs.

Keep It Authentic

Don't read from a script — understand the points and speak naturally.

Add Personal Touch

If they've had bad brand deal experiences, sharing that makes it more relatable.

Stay Platform-Native

Use trending audio for Reels, interactive stickers for Stories, carousel slides for Posts.
